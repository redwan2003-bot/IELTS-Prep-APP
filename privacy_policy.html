Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to IELTS Companion. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

2. Data Collection and Storage

IELTS Companion is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

  • Local Storage: All data, including your notes, flashcards, study progress, and uploaded documents, are stored entirely locally on your device using a secure local database.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking software to monitor your usage behavior.

3. Error Reporting

To improve the stability of the application, we may use an error reporting service (such as Sentry or Firebase Crashlytics) to collect anonymous crash logs. These logs only contain technical information about the crash and do not include any personally identifiable information or user-generated content.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
